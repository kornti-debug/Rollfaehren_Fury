The pitch

Tea party? No. Total mayhem.Project overview

Meet Captain Fraunz — the grumpiest, most stubbornly dedicated ferry operator on the river. When the local Danube wildlife mutates into relentless waves of adversaries, there's only one job left: keep the ferry afloat and get it to the other bank. Shoot, shout, and survive.

Defend the ferry from bank to bank — or sink trying.

How it plays

The core loopSurvive · loot · upgrade · repeat

01

Survive the waves

Endless, escalating rounds of increasingly ridiculous enemies. Shoot your way through and collect the cash and items they drop.

02

Spend at the vending machine

Between rounds, blow your cash on absurd augments at the onboard vending machine — like Greased Deck for faster movement.

03

Protect the hull and cargo

Fast, non-serious action with over-the-top physics. Watch for enemies that go straight for the boat — or your vital cargo.

⛴️ Player

Captain Fraunz

A grumpy but dedicated ferry captain. His tools are simple: he can shoot, and he can shout. Keep the ferry alive.

🕊️ Threat · Sky

Cursed Pöltener Pigeon

Wild, corrupted pigeons from St. Pölten that circle above the ferry and spread chaos with their filthy presence.

🐟 Threat · Water

Radioactive Tullner Tuna

Glowing fish from near Tulln, mutated by pollution and absolutely ready to ruin Captain Fraunz's day.

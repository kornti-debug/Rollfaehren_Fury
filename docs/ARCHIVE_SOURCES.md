## Pre-Project PDF

Local reference used during documentation:

```text
C:/Users/Luki/Desktop/Rollfaehre_PreProject.pdf
```

Key points extracted:

- FPS shooter protecting a ferry crossing the river.
- Survive enemy waves.
- Earn money from enemy kills.
- Earn extra money from surviving cargo.
- Spend money at a shop between rounds.
- Next round becomes harder and may include more cargo.
- Ferry destruction triggers game over.
- Planned systems: `GameManager`, `RoundManager`, `ScoreManager`, `UIManager`, `SpawnManager`, `ShopManager`, `HealthSystem`, `WeaponSystem`, `UpgradeSystem`, `FerryController`, `Cargo`.
